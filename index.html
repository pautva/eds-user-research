Who needs integrated access to environmental data?

The Problem: Environmental data is scattered across many data centres. Users struggle to find and integrate the data they need, and often don't know what's available.

Our Hypothesis: An integrated environmental data platform could address this issue. We're conducting user research using customer sprint methodology to understand if there is a need for such a platform and, if there is, what it should be and what features it should have.

This is a short summary of our work in progress, summarised as personas of user groups who could benefit from such a platform.

Get Involved: If you would like to participate in this research or provide feedback, contact us at ux@bgs.ac.uk

PERSONA_TYPE: TECHNICAL_USER
THE ACADEMIC RESEARCHER
Research Fellow • Lecturer • Professor • PhD Candidate
Seeks harmonised, multi-domain environmental datasets with programmatic access for high-impact research publications. Values reproducibility, comprehensive metadata, and standardised APIs.
Key Needs:
  • → APIs and Python/R libraries
  • → Analysis-ready datasets
  • → Jupyter notebooks with examples
  • → DOIs for dataset citation
PERSONA_TYPE: TECHNICAL_USER
THE APPLIED DATA SCIENTIST
Data Scientist • GIS/Geospatial Analyst • Research Data Analyst • ML Engineer
Delivers data-driven solutions to real-world environmental challenges. Builds predictive models and decision-support tools, bridging research and practical application.
Key Needs:
  • → Unified search across data centres
  • → Cloud-optimised formats
  • → Pre-processed, analysis-ready datasets
  • → Integration guides for industry tools
PERSONA_TYPE: TECHNICAL_USER
THE REMOTE SENSING & EO SPECIALIST
Remote Sensing Specialist • Earth Observation Scientist • Geospatial Engineer
Extracts environmental insights from satellite and sensor data. Develops automated monitoring systems and integrates Earth Observation data with ground-based measurements.
Key Needs:
  • → Integrated EO and ground-based datasets
  • → Cloud-based processing environments
  • → Analysis-ready data (ARD) products
  • → Validation datasets and workflows
