This project defines user personas for the Environmental Data Service (EDS), a comprehensive platform designed to provide seamless access to multi-domain environmental data. Through extensive user research and stakeholder interviews, we have identified key user archetypes that represent the diverse needs of our scientific community.

Each persona captures the unique workflows, pain points, and requirements of different user groups, enabling us to design data services that truly serve the environmental science community. From academic researchers conducting climate studies to policy makers requiring real-time environmental monitoring, these personas guide our product development and user experience decisions.

Key Focus Areas: Data discoverability, programmatic access, interoperability, metadata quality, and reproducible research workflows.

PERSONA_TYPE: TECHNICAL_USER
THE ACADEMIC RESEARCHER
Research Fellow • Lecturer • Professor • PhD Candidate
Seeks harmonised, multi-domain environmental datasets with programmatic access for high-impact research publications. Values reproducibility, comprehensive metadata, and standardised APIs.
Key Needs:
  • → APIs and Python/R libraries
  • → Analysis-ready datasets
  • → Jupyter notebooks with examples
  • → DOIs for dataset citation

PERSONA_TYPE: DECISION_MAKER
THE POLICY MAKER
Government Official • Environmental Consultant • NGO Leader
Requires timely, authoritative environmental data to inform policy decisions and regulatory frameworks. Needs clear visualisations and executive summaries rather than raw datasets.
Key Needs:
  • → Interactive dashboards
  • → Executive summaries
  • → Real-time monitoring alerts
  • → Regulatory compliance data

PERSONA_TYPE: COMMERCIAL_USER
THE INDUSTRY ANALYST
Environmental Consultant • Risk Analyst • Sustainability Manager
Integrates environmental data into commercial risk assessments and sustainability reporting. Requires reliable, up-to-date data with clear licensing and usage terms.
Key Needs:
  • → Commercial licensing options
  • → High-frequency data updates
  • → Integration with business tools
  • → Quality assurance metrics

PERSONA_TYPE: PUBLIC_USER
THE CITIZEN SCIENTIST
Educator • Community Volunteer • Environmental Advocate
Seeks accessible environmental data for education, community projects, and local environmental monitoring. Values user-friendly interfaces and clear explanations.
Key Needs:
  • → Intuitive web interfaces
  • → Educational resources
  • → Local/regional data focus
  • → Mobile-friendly access
